base_path=law-firms (vertical from database)

## site.com
- homepage

## site.com/[base_path]
- search by either category or city, lists popular categories and cities for refined search

## site.com/[base_path]/[category]
- shows results in the specified category across all locations, lists popular cities for refined search

## site.com/[base_path]/[category]/[state]
- shows results in the specified category and state, lists cities for refined search

## site.com/[base_path]/[category]/[city]/[state]
- shows results in the specified category and location

## site.com/[base_path]/[category]/[city]/[state]/[business_id]
- shows detailed profile for the specified business in the given category and location

## site.com/[base_path]/[state]
- shows results in the specified state, lists categories and cities for refined search

## site.com/[base_path]/[city]/[state]
- shows results in the specified city and state, lists categories for refined search

## site.com/[category]
- shows informational content about the specified category across all locations, links to articles within that category


## site.com/[category]/[article_slug]
- shows detailed article content for the specified article within the given category
